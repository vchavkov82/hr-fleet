## Overview

The ATS module lets you publish jobs, collect applications, move candidates through a pipeline, and use AI-powered CV screening. Main capabilities: job posting with title and description, candidate pipeline with Kanban stages, AI CV screening, and interview scheduling. See [Quickstart](/docs/getting-started/quickstart) to post your first job and [Onboarding](/docs/features/onboarding) when a candidate accepts.
