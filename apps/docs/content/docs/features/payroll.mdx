## Overview

The **Payroll** module supports payroll processing for Bulgaria: salary, tax and social security (NRA/NOI), payslips, and bank payment files.

## Main capabilities

- **Payroll runs** — Process monthly payroll with deductions and employer contributions.
- **Bulgarian tax and social security** — Calculations aligned with NRA/NOI rules.
- **Payslips** — Generate and store; employees can view in the portal.
- **Bank export** — Export payment files for your bank.
