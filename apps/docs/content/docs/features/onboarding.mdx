## Overview

The **Onboarding** module gives new hires a clear start: task checklists, welcome portal, e-signatures for documents, and optional equipment tracking.

## Main capabilities

- **Task checklists** — Templates (Pre-day 1, Day 1, 30/60/90 days) assigned to HR, manager, or new hire.
- **Welcome portal** — Company info, documents, and tasks for the new hire.
- **E-signatures** — Send documents for electronic signature and store signed copies.
- **Equipment and access** — Track laptops, access cards, and accounts (if enabled).
