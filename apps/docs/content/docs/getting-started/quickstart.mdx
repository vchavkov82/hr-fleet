## Introduction

Create your account, post your first job, and invite team members.

## Step 1: Create your account

Go to hr.bg and click Start free trial. Enter your work email and password. Complete the onboarding form.

## Step 2: Post your first job

Open Recruitment, Jobs, Create job. Fill in title, location, department, and description. Click Publish.

## Step 3: Invite your team

Go to Settings, Team, Invite user. Enter email and choose role: Admin, HR, Recruiter, or Hiring manager.
